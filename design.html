Our Design

3D Model

Our design uses a rocker-bogie suspension system with a hollow electronics body. Grouser wheels will provide traction.

The 3D printed prototype provides a solid foundation for future iterations, though wheels and electronics integration are still in progress.

Electronics

The rover prototype uses Arduino-based control systems with servos for steering and DC motors for movement.

A concept lighting model demonstrates how landscape illumination will function.

This demonstration cycles RGB LED combinations via button input, representing how the final system may behave.
